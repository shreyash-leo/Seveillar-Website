Think. Commit. Serve.

Seveillar Clinical Supplies Services Private Limited is an India-based private limited company established in 2015, specializing in end-to-end clinical trial supplies management solutions for global pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and CRO organizations. With a strong foundation in quality, compliance, and operational excellence, Seveillar supports complex clinical trials across multiple regions with efficiency and reliability.

Our capabilities span the complete clinical supply chain, including labeling, packaging, storage, distribution, and management of Investigational Medicinal Products (IMPs). Backed by robust infrastructure, experienced professionals, and technology-driven processes, we ensure seamless execution while maintaining the highest standards of regulatory compliance.

The Meaning Behind Seveillar: Inspired by a Greek-origin concept, “Seve” signifies help and “Illar” signifies people, symbolizing our commitment to supporting people through clinical research and healthcare advancement.

Our Global Network & Journey

Seveillar Clinical Supplies has established a robust and reliable global network to support the complex requirements of clinical trial supply management. Through strategic partnerships with qualified vendors, logistics providers, and industry specialists, we ensure seamless coordination and efficient delivery of clinical supplies across multiple regions.

Our journey since 2015 has been defined by regulatory excellence, client-first innovation, and operational integrity. We house one of the largest facilities for labeling, packaging, and storage of IMPs, enabling us to efficiently support clinical studies of varying scale and complexity.

Our Mission: To continue to be a preferred global strategic partner for pharmaceutical companies by delivering cost-effective, compliant, and innovative clinical supplies management solutions for global clinical trials.
