About Seveillar

Established in 2015, Seveillar Clinical Supplies Services Pvt. Ltd. is an India-based end-to-end clinical trial supply management company serving global pharma, biotech, and CRO organizations. Our mission: deliver cost-effective, compliant, and innovative clinical supplies management solutions for global clinical trials.

Backed by QP-certified and USFDA-audited facilities, we house one of the largest IMP labelling, packaging, and storage facilities. We are committed to quality, reliability, and operational excellence.
