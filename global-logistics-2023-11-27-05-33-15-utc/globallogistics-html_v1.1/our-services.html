Clinical Supplies Consultancy & Project Management

At Seveillar Clinical Supplies Services, our Clinical Supplies Consultancy & Project Management service is designed to support sponsors, biotechnology companies, and CROs in planning and executing efficient, compliant, and cost-effective clinical trial supply strategies.

With extensive experience in global clinical trials, our team provides strategic guidance, operational expertise, and end-to-end project oversight to ensure seamless clinical supply execution from study start-up to close-out.

Our Key Service Offerings:

  • Development of Clinical Supplies Management Strategy: We develop comprehensive Clinical Supplies Management Strategies for global clinical studies, aligned with protocol requirements, study timelines, and regulatory expectations.
  • Clinical Supply Chain Consultation: Our experts provide clinical supply chain consultation to design and implement efficient supply models, ensuring seamless coordination across manufacturing, packaging, storage, and distribution activities.
  • Consultation & Designing of Innovative Blinding Techniques: We offer consultation and design of innovative blinding techniques to support blinded clinical trials.
  • Strategic Clinical Supply Planning: We collaborate closely with sponsors and study teams to develop customized clinical supply strategies aligned with study design, timelines, and regulatory requirements.
  • End-to-End Project Management: Our dedicated project managers act as a single point of contact, coordinating all clinical supply activities, timelines, and stakeholders.
  • Risk Management & Mitigation: Our proactive approach identifies potential supply chain challenges early and implements mitigation strategies.
  • Global Trial Support: Seveillar supports multi-country and global clinical trials with cross-border supply management.

Clinical Supplies Manufacturing

Seveillar Clinical Supplies Services provides Clinical Supplies Manufacturing solutions designed to support global clinical trials with precision, compliance, and operational excellence. Our manufacturing capabilities are aligned with global regulatory standards and supported by robust infrastructure, experienced professionals, and quality-driven processes.

Our Manufacturing Capabilities:

  • Placebo & Active Dosage Form Manufacturing: Seveillar supports Active Dosage Form Manufacturing through qualified and compliant manufacturing processes. Manufacturing of placebo is carried out through approved third-party cGMP facilities, ensuring quality, consistency, and compliance.
  • Over Encapsulation: We provide Over Encapsulation services to support blinded clinical trials, ensuring uniformity between placebo and active products while maintaining product stability and quality.
  • Strategic Tie-Up with 3rd Party cGMP Contract Manufacturers: Through strategic partnerships with qualified third-party cGMP contract manufacturers, we extend our capabilities to support diverse dosage forms, scale-up requirements, and global clinical study needs while maintaining strict quality oversight.

Clinical Supplies Blinding, Packaging & Labelling

Seveillar Clinical Supplies Services provides comprehensive Clinical Supplies Blinding, Packaging & Labelling solutions designed to support global clinical trials with accuracy, compliance, and operational flexibility. Our services are tailored to meet protocol-specific requirements while ensuring regulatory compliance, product integrity, and study blinding throughout the clinical trial lifecycle.

Our Packaging & Labelling Capabilities:

  • Blinding Strategy Design & Implementation: Customized blinding strategies aligned with study protocols and trial objectives.
  • Primary Packaging of Non-Potent Oral Solid Dosage Forms: Tablets, capsules, lozenges under controlled conditions.
  • Secondary Packaging for All Dosage Forms: Temperature-controlled environments including Controlled Room Temperature (15°C to 25°C), Refrigerated (2°C to 8°C), and Frozen (-15°C to -25°C).
  • Clinical Supplies Label Design & Generation: Primary labels, secondary labels, booklet labels, multi-language labels, randomization labels, kit labels, temperature-specific labels, Expiry Date Extension Labels.
  • Protocol-Specific Packaging Solutions: Customized configurations based on study design, blinding requirements, distribution strategy, storage conditions, and country-specific regulations.

Storage, Distribution & Global Logistics Management

Seveillar Clinical Supplies Services provides comprehensive Storage, Distribution & Global Logistics Management solutions designed to ensure the safe handling, storage, and delivery of clinical trial supplies across global locations. Our services are built on robust infrastructure, regulatory compliance, and operational excellence to support seamless execution of clinical trials worldwide.

Our Storage & Distribution Capabilities:
